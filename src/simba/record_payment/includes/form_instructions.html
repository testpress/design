Record Partial Payment

Record a partial payment for the student's outstanding fee by completing the fields below:

  • Select Invoice: Choose the pending invoice to which the payment applies. Review the invoice date, total amount, and outstanding balance.
  • Amount: Enter the amount the student is paying as part of the fee.
  • Payment Method: Select the mode of payment.
  • Transaction Reference: If the payment is not in cash, provide the transaction ID. This field will appear only if a non-cash payment method is selected.
  • Note (Optional): Add any remarks or additional details regarding this payment.
  • Balance Due: The remaining fee amount will be automatically calculated and displayed after entering the payment amount.

Click "Record Payment" to submit the details and update the student's payment status.
