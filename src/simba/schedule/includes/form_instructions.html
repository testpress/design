Creating a new schedule

To create a new schedule, please fill out the following details:

  • Date: Enter the date in the format DD-MM-YYYY.
  • Time Slot: Select the time slot for the schedule.
  • Subject: Specify the subject to be taught.
  • Topic: Provide the topic that will be covered during the class.
  • Teacher: Choose the teacher responsible for the class.
  • Room: Specify the room where the schedule will take place.
  • Batches: Select at least one batch. This field is mandatory.

After filling out all the required fields, click the "Create Schedule" button to save the new entry.
