How Risk Levels Are Determined

Status is calculated from these fields: Average Score %, Avg Review, Follow-up Coverage, and At-Risk Students.

  • At Risk: (Average Score % < 55 AND Avg Review < 3.0) OR At-Risk Students >= 18 OR Follow-up Coverage < 40%
  • Needs Attention: not At Risk AND (Average Score % < 70 OR Avg Review < 3.5 OR Follow-up Coverage < 75% OR At-Risk Students >= 10)
  • Healthy: all other cases
