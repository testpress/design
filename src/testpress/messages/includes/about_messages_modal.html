Message

Have a private one-to-one conversation with a student. Only the student and admins can participate in the conversation.

Best for Student questions, support, personal follow-ups, and lead conversations.
Important behavior AI-generated lead alerts can also appear inside a student's direct conversation, allowing admins to follow up with the student directly.

Group

Create a shared conversation with multiple students. Members can participate and communicate in the same conversation.

Best for Batch discussions, study groups, doubt discussions, and collaborative conversations.
Important behavior Admins can add students individually or include students through selected batches and courses. Group membership can be managed later.

Broadcast

Send the same message to many students without creating a shared group conversation. Recipients do not see or interact with each other.

Best for Fee reminders, course promotions, event reminders, and targeted communication.
Important behavior The recipient list is fixed for the broadcast. Students do not reply inside the shared broadcast thread; any supported replies should be handled privately.

Announcement

Share important or official information with a selected audience.

Best for Holiday notices, exam schedules, policy updates, class changes, and important institute updates.
Important behavior Announcements are primarily for one-way sharing of information. Students cannot reply to announcements.
