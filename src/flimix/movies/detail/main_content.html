Jailer

2008 • 2h 32m • Action • Crime • Drama • U/A 13+

Muthuvel Pandian, a retired jailer, lives a peaceful life with his family. When his son, a sincere police officer, goes missing while investigating an idol smuggling mafia, Muthuvel sets out to find the truth. What follows is a gripping tale of revenge, identity, and justice.
