How to read the content discovery section

This chart shows whether viewers tend to explore new content or return to titles they have already watched. It helps understand how well the platform encourages content discovery. Use the patterns below to interpret the chart.

Pattern Chart What this means What to do
New Titles Watched is much higher than Rewatched Titles Viewers are actively exploring new content across the platform. Continue promoting new releases and discovery features.
Rewatched Titles is much higher than New Titles Watched Many viewers prefer watching familiar titles instead of discovering new content. Highlight new content on the homepage and improve recommendations to encourage discovery.
Both bars are similar Viewers both explore new content and revisit titles they like. Maintain balance; promote both new releases and continue-watching.
Both values are low Overall viewing activity during the selected time period is low. Review marketing, content availability, and platform reach.
No data appears There was no content viewing activity during the selected time range. Verify tracking and that content was available in the period.
