General Test Information

This is a test of your ability to use English in an academic context. There are four sections: Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing.

Answer every question to the best of your ability. Note that some Reading and Listening questions are worth 2 points as indicated on the test. When answering questions in the Speaking and Writing sections, be sure to use your own words. Do not include memorized reasons or examples in your responses.

You make take notes to help you answer the test questions and prepare your responses. Your notes will not be scored.

There will be directions before each section. You may dismiss these directions at any time by selecting Continue.

During this practice test, you may select Pause Test at any time. This will stop the test until you decide to continue. You may continue the test at any time during the period that your test is activated. When taking the actual test, pausing the test will not be available.

Select Return to go on.
