Test Rules

Disruptive behavior will not be permitted at the test center. The Test Administrator will determine what constitutes disruptive behavior. The Test Administrator is authorized to dismiss you from the test session for disruptive behavior and to notify Pearson VUE of the action taken.

If at any time during the exam you need assistance, or if there is a problem with your computer or workstation, raise your hand to alert the Test Administrator.

Please review the GMAT Online exam day testing procedures on mba.com. Failure to comply with these could result in your exam being terminated, your score being cancelled, and/or other consequences, at the sole discretion of GMAC.
