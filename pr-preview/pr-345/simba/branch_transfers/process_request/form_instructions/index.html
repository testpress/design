Processing a Branch Transfer Request

Complete the following steps to process the branch transfer request:

  • Payment Verification: Confirm whether a transfer fee is applicable and collect payment if required.
  • Transfer Fee Details: Ensure the fee is calculated based on the enrollment duration:
    • Within 60 Days: No transfer fee.
    • After 60 Days: Collect the standard transfer fee.
  • Payment Recording: Record the payment details, including the payment method and the transaction ID.

Click "Process Request" to finalize the payment process and move the request forward.
