Processing a Branch Transfer Request

Follow these steps to process a branch transfer request:

  • Transfer Fee: Check the applicable transfer fee based on the student’s enrollment date. If the transfer is requested after 60 days, ensure payment is recorded before proceeding.
  • Preferred Branch: The branch the student wishes to transfer to.
  • Reason for Transfer: Provide a valid reason, e.g., relocation or schedule preference.
  • Conditions:
    • Photo Upload: Ensure the student’s photo is uploaded in the system.
    • Outstanding Amount: Confirm that all previous payments are cleared.
  • Comments: Additional notes or context for the transfer.

Click "Submit Request" to finalize.
