Boost Your Sales with WhatsApp Checkout Reminders

You can now recover abandoned sales automatically via WhatsApp reminders. Don’t let your students slip away!

Know More

WhatsApp Checkout Reminders

What’s New: We’ve introduced WhatsApp Abandoned Checkout Reminders to help you recover potential lost sales. If a student adds a course to their cart but doesn’t complete the purchase, they will automatically receive reminder messages on WhatsApp, encouraging them to finish their checkout process.

Why It’s Important: Research shows that 70% of students who start the checkout process don’t complete the payment. This feature automates the recovery of those sales without any additional effort from your end.

Key Features:
  • Reminder after 2 hours: A gentle reminder is sent to students who haven’t completed their purchase.
  • Reminder after 16 hours: A second reminder is sent to nudge them further to complete the checkout process.
  • Each reminder includes a deep link that directs the student back to the course page, making it easy for them to complete their purchase.
How It Works:
  • Once enabled, the system automatically tracks students who abandon their cart before completing payment.
  • Students will receive two WhatsApp reminders: one after 2 hours and another after 16 hours.
  • If the student is already logged in, they will be taken straight to checkout. If not, they will be prompted to log in first.
Why It Matters:
  • Automated Sales Recovery: This feature works as an automated follow-up tool, saving you time and effort in contacting students manually.
  • Higher Conversion Rate: Students who abandon their carts are more likely to return and complete their purchase after receiving these reminders.
  • Reduced Overhead: With WhatsApp reminders handling the follow-up, you can focus more on other aspects of your business.
How to Enable:

To activate WhatsApp Abandoned Checkout Reminders, please reach out to our support team. We'll assist you with the setup. You can contact us at support@testpress.in.
